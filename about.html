About

We're commited to helping families in need of food in our church community. Age, gender, race, nationality and personal circumstance are not factors in your ability to get food. We stock baked beans, pasta, hearty low sodium soups, and other canned goods. Due to the risk of COVID-19, all visitors are required to wear a mask.

If you would like information on foodbanks and volunteering, please visit foodbank.org.
