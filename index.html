News

Food pickups are on the first Sunday of every month. Until the pandemic ends, food pickups will be held in front of Cathedral Church of Saint Paul at 19 Chatham St in Worcester, MA.

During the pandemic, we are providing a pre-boxed assortment of groceries including cold cereal, canned vegetables, canned fruit, pasta, mac & cheese, snacks, frozen meats, cheese, eggs, toilet paper, paper towels, tissues, soap, and other miscellaneous items.
